What We Do

Our Services

A new roof is one of the biggest investments you can make on your property. We'll make sure you get it right — the first time.

01
Residential Roofing

New roof installations for homes across Tauranga and the Bay of Plenty. We tailor each solution to your property, using premium COLORSTEEL® materials rated for NZ conditions.

02
Commercial Roofing

Robust commercial roof solutions for businesses, offices and industrial buildings. Minimal disruption. Maximum durability. On time, every time.

03
Roof Repairs

Leaks, storm damage, worn flashings — our team identifies the cause correctly and fixes it properly. We'll tell you if you need a repair, not a full re-roof.

04
Re-Roofing

Complete roof replacement for homes and buildings. We only remove what we can replace same day — your property stays weather-tight throughout the process.

05
Insulation

We install quality insulation while your roof is open — the most cost-effective time to do it. Keep your home warm, dry and energy-efficient year-round.

06
Roof Inspections

Pre-purchase and maintenance inspections — we give you an honest assessment of what you're working with, including clear photos and transparent recommendations.

About Us

Andre Quellin
& the Team

Mount Roof Services is locally owned and operated, proudly servicing Tauranga, Papamoa, Mount Maunganui, Te Puke and the wider Bay of Plenty.

Led by Andre Quellin, our team is known for straight-talking advice, precision workmanship, and the kind of service that turns first-time customers into long-term clients. We don't upsell — if your carport doesn't need a new roof, we'll tell you.

Every job is backed by comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship. We use premium COLORSTEEL® — tested to NZ standards, built for our coastal climate.
